About Mbeya Avocados

Mbeya Avocados is a green-driven agricultural enterprise located in the fertile Southern Highlands of Tanzania. We specialize in producing premium avocados while empowering smallholder farmers and protecting the environment.

Our farms benefit from high-altitude volcanic soils, ideal climate conditions, and sustainable agricultural practices that ensure quality, traceability, and long-term environmental stewardship.

Our Community

We work directly with individual growers and organized farmer groups from the Mount Rungwe region, creating reliable market access and sustainable income streams for rural families.
